## What Is It?

**Migrate My AI Chat** is a Chrome extension that lets you instantly move entire conversations between ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Copilot, DeepSeek, Perplexity, and Xiaomi MiMo — with full context, in one click.

No more copy-pasting 47 messages one by one. No more re-explaining your project from scratch. Your conversations follow you wherever you go.

## How It Works

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1** | Open any AI chat (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) |
| **2** | Click the extension icon and hit **Export** |
| **3** | Click **Continue On** and pick your destination AI |
| **4** | Your new AI picks up exactly where you left off |

That's it. No accounts. No server uploads. Your data never leaves your browser.
